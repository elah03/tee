Generic name:Neomycin,Polymyxin B and Gramicidin

Dosage:Opthalamic solution

mild side effects

  • Itching
  • skin rash
  • redness
  • swelling

Storage

Store the medicine in a closed container at room temperature,

away from heat,moisture and direct light.Keep from freezing.

Keep out reach of children

Do not keep outdated medicine or medicine no longer needed.

Ask your healthcare professional how you should dispose of any medicine you do not use.

Precautions

  • It is very important that your doctor check your progress while your are using this medicine.
  • This medicine can not be used for treatment of a heart attack.Use an immediate-release aspirin in such an event.
  • Make sure your doctor knows if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. Do not use this medicine during the later part of the pregnancy unless your doctor tells you to.
  • This medicine may increase risk of bleeding problems,including stomach ulcers or bleeding.
  • Do not take other medicines unless they have been discussed with your doctor.This includes prescription or nonprescription(over the counter[OTC]medicines and herbal or vitamin supplements.
